Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2025

Simple Display Color Checker is committed to protecting your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app runs entirely offline. It does not require or use an internet connection, and no user data is transmitted or stored.

Simple Display Color Checker does not use third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or tracking tools. All included libraries and plugins are open-source and used solely to support the app’s offline functionality (e.g., screen brightness control and fullscreen mode).

The only permissions used are related to screen display settings, which are strictly required for display testing purposes.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us.
