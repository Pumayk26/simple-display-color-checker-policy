Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2025

Simple Display Color Checker does not collect, store, or share any personal information. This app functions entirely offline and does not require or access the internet.

No user data is transmitted, tracked, or analyzed in any way. The app does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.

The only permissions used are screen-related features such as fullscreen mode and brightness control, strictly to support display testing functionality.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to contact me.
